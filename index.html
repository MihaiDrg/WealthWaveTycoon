About the game

An empire that grows whether you're watching or not.

WealthWave Idle Tycoon is an idle business simulation game about turning nothing into everything. Every session starts the same way: tap to earn your first coin. From there, the game becomes a study in compounding — reinvest what you make, and let it start making more without you.

Buy your first investments, then use the income they generate to unlock new businesses, from small local shops to sprawling international brands. Diversify into real estate to stabilize your growth, and once you've pushed a run as far as it can go, prestige it away for a permanent multiplier through the Legacy Vault and Ascension systems. Progress never really resets — it transforms.

And because wealth doesn't sleep, neither does your empire: offline earnings keep working in the background, so every time you open the app there's more waiting for you than when you left.

Everything in the loop

Features built for long-term growth.

Eleven systems, one compounding loop — designed to reward both a quick tap between meetings and a long strategic session.

Tap Income

Every tap earns money instantly — the simplest way to keep momentum going during a quick break.

Passive Income

Set up income streams that keep paying out on their own, so your empire grows even between sessions.

Investment System

Put your capital to work across a range of investments, each with its own risk, return and pace.

Business Empire

Unlock and upgrade dozens of businesses, from a first small shop to a multinational holding.

Real Estate

Diversify into property to stabilize your income and unlock a steadier, longer-term growth curve.

Prestige

Reset your run at the right moment to convert progress into a permanent multiplier for the next one.

Legacy Vault

Bank the value of every prestige into a vault of permanent bonuses that carry across every future run.

Ascension

Push past prestige into Ascension tiers, unlocking mechanics and multipliers standard runs never reach.

Offline Progress

Close the app and your businesses keep earning, so there's always a payout waiting when you return.

Achievements

Work toward milestones that track your growth and unlock recognition for major turning points.

Rewarded Bonuses

Claim optional bonus boosts to accelerate a session whenever you want an extra push forward.
